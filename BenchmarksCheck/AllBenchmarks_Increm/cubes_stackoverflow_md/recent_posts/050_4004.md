| 4 | B | 5 | Yahoo | 300 |

# solution

```sql
SELECT t1.role_id,
       t1.role_type,
       t1.ref_id,
       COALESCE(e.employee_name, o.org_name) AS ref_name,
       COALESCE(e.joining_date, o.org_max)   AS ref_joining_date
FROM "050_1" AS t1
         LEFT JOIN "050_2" AS e
                   ON e.employee_id = t1.ref_id
                       AND t1.role_type = 'A'
         LEFT JOIN "050_3" AS o
                   ON o.org_id = t1.ref_id
                       AND t1.role_type = 'B'
```
