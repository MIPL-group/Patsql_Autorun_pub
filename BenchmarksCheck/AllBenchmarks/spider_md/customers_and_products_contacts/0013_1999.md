# solution

```sql
SELECT T1.customer_name
FROM customers AS T1
JOIN customer_orders AS T2 ON T1.customer_id = T2.customer_id
WHERE T1.payment_method_code = 'Credit Card'
GROUP BY T1.customer_id
HAVING count(*) > 2
```
