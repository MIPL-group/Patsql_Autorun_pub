# solution

```sql
SELECT city
FROM addresses
WHERE city NOT IN
    (SELECT DISTINCT t3.city
     FROM customers AS t1
     JOIN customer_addresses AS t2 ON t1.customer_id = t2.customer_id
     JOIN addresses AS t3 ON t2.address_id = t3.address_id)
```
